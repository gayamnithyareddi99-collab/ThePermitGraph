Everything you need to know about ThePermitGraph and what it means for your organisation's permit efficiency and regulatory compliance in the AI-powered era.

What is ThePermitGraph and why does my business need it?

+
ThePermitGraph is an AI-powered permit and compliance intelligence platform that solves the systemic operational crisis facing UK developers, contractors, and regulated businesses specifically the "Regulatory Opacity" where permit dependencies, compliance conditions, and enforcement risks are invisible until they cause expensive project delays or fines. Unlike standard compliance trackers that serve as passive deadline lists, ThePermitGraph establishes a live Permit Intelligence Graph that automatically maps every regulatory dependency, monitors every active permit, and alerts teams to every compliance risk in real time. Pilot clients have achieved 94% faster permit mapping and zero compliance penalties.

How is this different from traditional planning consultants or compliance software?

+
Traditional planning consultants charge £500–£5,000 per day and deliver periodic, static advice. Traditional compliance software maintains static databases updated quarterly. ThePermitGraph operates as a live AI intelligence layer continuously updated from authoritative regulatory sources that automatically maps permit dependencies, tracks compliance conditions, scores enforcement risk, and automates deadline workflows in real time. It is architecturally designed to complement, not replace, your legal team by handling the intelligence work so your advisors can focus on advocacy.

Which jurisdictions and permit types does ThePermitGraph cover?

+
ThePermitGraph currently covers 40+ UK and international jurisdictions including all UK local planning authorities, the Environment Agency, building control bodies, and key sector-specific regulators (Ofgem, HSE, CQC, FCA, and more). Permit types covered include planning permission, listed building consent, environmental permits, building regulations approval, HMO licences, premises licences, street works permits, and a growing library of sector-specific consents. New jurisdictions and permit types are added based on client demand and regulatory change.

Is ThePermitGraph compliant with UK data protection regulations?

+
Yes. ThePermitGraph is architecturally designed to comply with UK GDPR, ICO guidance on AI and Data Protection, and the UK's Principles-Based AI Framework. All client project data is stored on UK-based infrastructure, encrypted at rest and in transit, and never shared with third parties or used to train models without explicit consent. Regulatory intelligence data is sourced exclusively from publicly available authoritative sources with full attribution and audit trails.

What sectors and business types does ThePermitGraph serve?

+
ThePermitGraph is purpose-built for residential and commercial property developers, construction contractors, infrastructure delivery organisations, energy and utilities companies, hospitality operators, healthcare providers, and any UK business operating in a regulated environment. The platform is particularly powerful for organisations managing multiple concurrent projects across different local authority areas where permit complexity and compliance risk multiply rapidly without intelligent management.
